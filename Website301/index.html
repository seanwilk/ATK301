About Me

My name is Sean Wilkinson. I am 23 years old and a junior at Illinois State University. This website is an assignemnt for my web design class.

My hobbies span all over the place, ranging from playing and listening to music, photography, playing tennis, to making jewelry, sound design, and live video for concerts.

I began playing music in the fourth grade, starting with playing cello in the school orchestra.
I played cello until sophomore year of high school, when I switched to upright bass. Bass lead me to playing on the school jazz band, and i also played in my friend's rock band.


I also play guitar, piano, ukulele, and a little bit of drums. Currently I am working on making electronic music. I am interested in pursuing sound design and foley work as a profession.
